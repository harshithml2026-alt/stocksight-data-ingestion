Item 7.01.       Regulation FD Disclosure

On November 24, 2015, the Honorable John C. Coughenour entered a Preliminary Approval Order (“Order”) approving the Stipulation and Agreement of Settlement in Barovic v. Ballmer, et al., No. 2:14-cv-00540-JCC, a shareholder derivative action in the United States District Court for the Western District of Washington. Pursuant to the Order, we are furnishing the attached Notice of Pendency and Proposed Settlement of Shareholder Derivative Action to holders of record and beneficial owners of the common stock of Microsoft Corporation.
