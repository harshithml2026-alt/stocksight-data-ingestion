Item 2.02 Results of Operations and Financial Condition.

On  February 16, 2011, NVIDIA Corporation issued a press release announcing its results for the three months and fiscal year ended January 30, 2011. The press release is attached as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated herein by reference.

Attached hereto as Exhibit 99.2 and incorporated by reference herein is financial information and commentary by David L. White, Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of NVIDIA, regarding results of the quarter ended January 30, 2011, or the CFO Commentary.  The CFO Commentary will be posted to www.nvidia.com/ir immediately after the filing of this Current Report.
