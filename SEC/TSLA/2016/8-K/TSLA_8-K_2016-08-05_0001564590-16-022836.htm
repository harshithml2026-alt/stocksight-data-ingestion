Item 8.01Other Events. 

 

In footnote 1B to the Condensed Consolidated Statement of Operations included in the Second Quarter 2016 Update that was furnished as Exhibit 99.1 to Tesla Motors, Inc.’s (the “Company”) Current Report on Form 8-K dated August 3, 2016 (the “Q2 Letter”), the amount of the “Net increase in operating lease vehicles as result of lease accounting and therefore not recognized in automotive cost of sales” for the three and six months ended June 30, 2016 was incorrectly stated (in thousands) as: $97,247 and $456,345, respectively. The correct amounts (in thousands) are: $241,253 and $600,351, respectively.

 

This error was limited to the above-referenced footnote that was voluntarily provided as supplemental information and that is not required by U.S. generally accepted accounting principles, and did not impact the cost of revenues reported in the Condensed Consolidated Statement of Operations or the Reconciliation of GAAP to Non-GAAP Financial Information included in the Q2 Letter.
